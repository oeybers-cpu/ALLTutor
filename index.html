I need help formatting a legal claim for a personal injury case in California. 10:05 AM
Under California law, a personal injury claim should include:
  1. Plaintiff and Defendant information
  2. Statement of jurisdiction
  3. Factual allegations (with specific dates)
  4. Legal claims (e.g., negligence)
  5. Damages sought
Example Format:
"Plaintiff, by and through undersigned counsel, alleges:
1. Defendant was negligent in maintaining the premises.
2. On or about January 15, 2023, Plaintiff fell due to wet floor conditions.
3. Plaintiff suffered physical injuries and emotional distress."
